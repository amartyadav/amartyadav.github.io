Projects

The Blind Stick

This was my first project with Arduino. An upgrade to an usual walking stick, the Blind Stick has two ultrasonic emitters and receivers, one on the front and one at the bottom of the stick, enclosed securely. On the principle of SONAR, the sensors could immediately calculate the distance between the person carrying the stick and any obstacle in the front and/or below. If the distance between the two crossed the safe threshold, say 50 cm, a buzzer would fire up. And say if the person is in a loud area, then to make up for the buzzer alarm, a vibrating motor would strongly vibrate the stick, and thus warning the person of the obstacle.



Mission Info

The Mission Info is an Alexa skill developed in a 12 hour hackathon by me, alongwith my friend Lyuben. It uses the SpaceX dataset for the launches of their rockets. The dataset contains the launch data, launch site, the customer of the launch, and the rocket which was launched. An user can interact with the Mission Info skill in Alexa on their Echo Devices by triggering it by saying, "Alexa, start Mission Info". This will start the Mission Info skill. Then to use the skill to get the information for all the launches in a particular month of some year, the user can say things like, "Give me the launch info for January in 2014". Alexa will then respond by giving the launch date, launch site, customer for the launch, and the rocket's name.
Currently, this skill can only handle requests for the previous SpaceX launches. It is in its alpha stage for now. We are currently working to make this skill fetch the data from the dataset automatically as the datasets get updated. It will have more functionality in the future.
